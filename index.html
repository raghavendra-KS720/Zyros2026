About ZYROS

⚡

ZYROS 2026 is the flagship Inter-Collegiate Tech, Management, and Cultural Fest of Dayananda Sagar Business Academy (DSBA), bringing together talent, innovation, and creativity on a vibrant platform. Students from various colleges come together to showcase their skills, compete, and celebrate the spirit of collaboration and youthful energy.

Scheduled on 8th and 9th of April 2026, the fest features an exciting mix of technical, managerial and cultural events, including innovation challenges, strategic competitions, and energetic performances in music and dance.

This event goes beyond competition — it's a celebration of energy, talent, and experience. While the contests bring out the competitive spirit, the evening will come alive with an electrifying concert, making the entire event truly unforgettable.

General Protocol

The code of conduct for all warriors of ZYROS

⚖️
I
Participants must carry and wear their valid college ID card at all times throughout the event.
II
Participants must maintain respectful behavior. Any abuse, fights, or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
III
Participants must report to the venue at least 30 minutes before their scheduled event.
IV
The decision of the judges will be final and binding in all matters of competition.
V
Any damage to property may lead to fines or disqualification at the discretion of the organizing committee.
VI
The decision of the organizing committee will be final in case of any disputes or disagreements.
VII
On-spot registration must be completed before 9:30 AM on the day of the event.
VIII
Participants must carry a backup of their audio track in a pendrive for all performance-based events.
